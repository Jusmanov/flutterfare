Comparing common transportation costs in NYC

Flutterfare compares cost estimates for different modes of transportation (only for NYC) based on fare information provided by the NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission and citibike. Users can adjust various factors that determine the cost of either medium of transport such as time of ride, type of bike, etc.

about

What is Flutterfare?

With various ways to go around New York City, one may want to weigh their transportation options based on price range, time efficiency, safety, and accessibility. Flutterfare is a website designed to present and compare these options, currently limited to citibike and yellow cab taxis with support for the NYC metro system (MTA data) arriving by 2025. It was created in 2024 by John Usmanov, once an NY resident.
